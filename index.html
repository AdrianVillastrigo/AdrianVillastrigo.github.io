Adrián Villastrigo

Working on macroevolutionary patterns

Barcelona (Spain)

Biography

My research has been focused on the evolutionary patterns of habitat transitions in aquatic Coleoptera, combining molecular biology, ecology and taxonomy. I am interested in understanding how biodiversity is assembly and the evolutionary processes behind it.

I am currently working in unveiling cryptic diversity linked to coastal habitats.
